All Things Unto Me Are Spiritual...

I love the scriptures and I love how true its truths are. I hope that makes sense, because I can’t say it any better way. You know that feeling you get when you see a truth taught in the Book of Mormon demonstrated in your life? It’s a feeling that expands your soul. I also get this feeling when I recognize an eternal truth being taught somewhere outside of scripture. I believe that the Lord inspires good people to understand precious heavenly truths and those people can be seen sharing bits and pieces of these truths throughout literature, history, science and art.

Now, Let me say a little bit about this website. I am not making this website to explain every little detail I’ve come across in my scripture studies. I’ve got a great notebook for that and it’s working just fine. The problem with my notebook is it doesn’t store videos or audio very well. So I created this site to store other notes that fare better in a digital format. You may find on this site, audio recordings of songs I’ve written that I think capture the mood of a particular chapter of scripture. Or you may find references to works of literature that I believe effectively illustrate a doctrinal principle. Anything that ,ay have enhanced my study and appreciation of the Book of Mormon is fair game to include on this site. So, that being said, this site is mostly just for me. However, I hope others are uplifted by this content as well. Everything I include here will primarily be centered on scripture (specifically the Book of Mormon) so feel free to roam around and discover some of my thoughts about and appreciation for the revealed word of God.

Overall I have 4 simple goals for this website:

  1. Provide an easy way to reference cool and applicable materials I have come across in my Book of Mormon study.
  2. Provie a space for storing content I have created about the Book of Mormon
    • Videos
    • Songs
    • Diagrams
  3. Provide me with a way to schedule my scripture study and be more accountable with it.
  4. Help me log spiritual impressions I have had in my study

thanks.
