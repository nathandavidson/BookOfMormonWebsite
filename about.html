About Me

Musician. BYU Student. Follower of Jesus.

I'm the one on the left. In reality, I am the same height as my Dad (the guy in the middle) but for whatever reason, I appear to be be a good 8 inches taller than him in this photo. Maybe that's why I like it.

At 21 years old, I am the oldest of my five other siblings and at 4 minutes and 57 seconds, I am the slowest Mario Kart Racer of my five siblings. I've got a different brand of hand-eye coordination called music. I have a passion for songwriting and the main instruments I write on are the guitar and the piano. I've been writing and recording for 3 years now and even have a few records I've released on streaming platforms like Spotify.

Nine Months ago I returned home from serving two years, full-time as a representative for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints. It was there I developed a relationship with Jesus Christ and a love of serving others. I am currently studying at Brigham Young University and plan to go on to dental school after I graduate. In my free time I enjoy disc golf, basketball, and spending time with my family.
